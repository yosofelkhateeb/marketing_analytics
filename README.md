# Marketing Campaign Analytics
End-to-end marketing analytics project using Python, SQL, and Power BI to analyze funnel performance and ROI.


## Business Poeblem
Marketing teams are able to collect tons of data from published campaigns; however, some struggle with interpretting such data into useful insights that can drive the campaign's direction. This analysis shall provide clear vision into which channels and campaigns shall the team focus on, and where to allocate the budget based on derived KPIs.

## Objectives
- Analyze marketing funnel performance
- Evaluate channel and campaign efficiency
- Identify areas to optimize spend

## KPIs 
- Impressions
- Clicks
- Conversions
- Conversion Rate
- Cost per Acquisition (CPA)
- Retrn on Investment (ROI)
- Return on Ad Spend (ROAS)
- Spend by Channel
- Funnel Drop-off
